Gruppe 1

The union of Sabia. Built upon the bountiful nature, darker times and leadership of its past, this union is now among the most blessed countries in its corner of the world. Their food production, industry and housing are among its current greatest strengths. Unfortunately they lack a little in infrastructure and literacy. Sabia is a democratic union. There are fairly strong opposing groups against the current leadership and they slowly gain more and more public interest. However, the current greatest threat to the nation is a failed harvest, but the current leadership is unable to solve this issue. The people of Sabia are jolly. They live wonderful lives and while infrastructure might be lacking, their food production helps relief most of their issues. Religion holds no importance at all in their lives and, if anything, has made them more creative. The people of Sabia aren't spiritual at all either and they rely more on the world around them and each other.

Albin Mikal Hansen

Light green, well groomed hair hangs over a fresh, anguished face. Bright brown eyes, set rooted within their sockets, watch fondly over the ancestors they've disassociated with for so long. A moustache alluringly compliments his cheekbones and leaves a compelling memory of his upbringing. This is the face of Ninleyn Dawnfury, a true romancer among night elves. He stands big among others, despite his heavy frame. There's something enticing about him, perhaps it's a feeling of delight or perhaps it's simply his persistence. But nonetheless, people tend to ask him about his latest victory, while treating him to a good meal when he's around.

A birthmark reaching from the bottom of the right cheekbone , running towards his upper lip and ending on his right cheek leaves a grievous memory of lost honor. Golden, long hair hangs over a sculpted, gloomy face. Expressive aquamarine eyes, set rooted within their sockets, watch faithfully over the haven they've stood guard for for so long. A sword left a mark stretching from the left side of the forehead , running across the nose and ending on his forehead leaves a tormenting burden of deceased love. This is the face of Almon Moonfall, a true pioneer among night elves. He stands tall above others, despite his big frame. There's something bizarre about him, perhaps it's his friendly demeanor or perhaps it's simply his tenderness.
